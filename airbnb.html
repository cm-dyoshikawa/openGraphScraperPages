A cute boutique studio located on arguably the best street in Chelsea. A block from the Highline, endless Galleries and Restaurants, the wonderful Chelsea Market and the lively Meatpacking District. 1 Ave from the A-C-E subway and just blocks from the L to Brooklyn!

Description

The Space

The apartment has 13' high ceilings, exposed brick, an open kitchen with everything you'll need, beautiful high windows offering southern exposure, a very cozy and airy space. The apartment has amazing energy and is loved by many.

I do not have TV but my desktop computer is available for listening to music, watching Movies and TV shows with Wifi in the home. The shower has great water pressure. 
And the kitchen has all new appliances. 

Please note, I live here so the home is not completely empty.

Guest Access

I do not have TV but my desktop computer is available for listening to music, watching Movies and TV shows with Wifi in the home.

Interaction with Guests

I'm happy to answer any questions you may have. I'm here to help make your stay in NYC a memorable one.
Out of respect for my neighbors and my building, do prefer people to have reviews. Thank you for understanding.

The Neighborhood

This neighborhood is blossoming... On either avenue you have at least 4 noteworthy restaurants within a block not to mention the endless options in Meatpacking and the West Village. Just a block or two from here... Cookshop, Tia Pol, Fonda, Bottino, to name a few.
You are close proximity to the West Side Hwy and Highline (the old train track elevated park) are unbeatable any time of year. The Chelsea area for fantastic for wandering; weave in and out of the Galleries, shop or dine in the Meatpacking, hit Chelsea Market for a quick bite.... This area offers so much for you to do and see.

Getting Around

A-C-E is one Ave away on 8th Ave. Taxis are available on 9th ave going downtown and 10th ave going uptown. However, walking from here in any direction is really wonderful.

House Rules

I love my neighbors and my neighbors love me. Please don't abuse the building or the space. I would really appreciate adults that will respect my home to rent my apartment.
Please be mindful of the echo in the hallway. We try and refrain from talking in there due to how the sound carries.
Many thanks!

Hello,

My name is Briana and I'm originally from Seattle. I have lived in Manhattan for over 7 years now and I've been lucky enough to call the beautiful Chelsea area my home.
Some of the things I love about the area on the weekend are; Walking out my front door to a quiet tree lined street and grabbing coffee at the Highline Hotel. Then sitting at the bar at Cookshop for brunch, followed by a stroll through the shop next door, 'This is Story'. From here, continuing on down to the Meatpacking, and shopping Bleecker street. In the late afternoon, throwing the frisbee in the park and hitting the Highline at dusk to avoid crowds...

During the week days my Team and I are running our Relocation and Interior Design business.

I hope you enjoy your stay in the city!
